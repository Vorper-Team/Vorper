# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: August 4, 2024**

This Privacy Policy explains how Vorper ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you visit our website located at [www.vorper.com](https://www.vorper.com) ("Website"). By using our Website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## Collection Information

We collect a variety of information to help us understand how our Website is being used and to improve our services. The information we collect includes:

- **Pages Visited**: Information about the pages you visit and the content you view.
- **Time Spent on Pages**: Data regarding how long you spend on each page.
- **Basic Technical Data**: Information such as browser type, operating system, referral URLs, and other similar data that helps us understand your browsing context.

We use Google Analytics to collect and analyze this technical information. Google Analytics uses cookies and other tracking technologies to provide insights into how visitors interact with our Website. The data collected is anonymized and aggregated to help us make informed decisions about our Website's performance and user experience.

## Purpose of Data Collection

The primary purposes for collecting this data are as follows:

- **Improving Our Services**: The information we gather helps us understand which parts of our Website are most popular and which areas may need improvement. This enables us to provide a better user experience for our visitors.
- **Content Optimization**: By analyzing user behavior, we can optimize our content to ensure that it is relevant and engaging to our audience.
- **No Marketing Use**: We emphasize that we do not use the collected data for marketing purposes. Our sole intention is to improve the educational content and documentation provided on our Website.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We take your privacy seriously and do not intentionally share your personal data with third parties. However, certain data may be processed by third-party services that we use to operate our Website:

- **Google Analytics**: This service helps us analyze Website traffic and usage patterns. Google Analytics may collect data such as your IP address, but we do not have direct access to this information.
- **GitHub Pages**: Our Website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect certain technical information as part of their hosting services.

## No Intentional Disclosure

We do not purposely disclose your personal information to any third parties for their marketing purposes. Any sharing of data that occurs is incidental to the services provided by the third parties mentioned above.

## Data Retention

We retain user data for a period of 60 days. This duration allows us to gather sufficient insights to make meaningful improvements to our Website and services. After this period, the data is either deleted or anonymized to protect your privacy.

## User Rights

We respect your rights regarding your personal data and provide you with the following rights and controls:

- **Right to Access**: You have the right to request access to the personal information we have collected about you. This allows you to understand what data we hold and verify that we are processing it in accordance with the law.

- **Right to Deletion**: You also have the right to request the deletion of your personal information. If you wish to exercise this right, please contact us at [contact@vorper.com](mailto:contact@vorper.com). We will take reasonable steps to delete your data from our records unless retention is required by law.

## Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your personal data. To protect your information, we implement a variety of security measures:

- **Encryption**: We use modern encryption technologies, including HTTPS, to secure data transmitted between your device and our Website. This helps prevent unauthorized access to your data during transmission.

- **Hosting Security**: Our Website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which provides robust security measures to protect user data. GitHub Pages implements industry-standard security practices, including regular security audits and updates.

## Cookies and Tracking

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. We use cookies to enhance your experience on our Website:

### Types of Cookies

- **Session Cookies**: These cookies store your light/dark mode preference, ensuring that your chosen settings are applied each time you visit our Website.
- **Analytics Cookies**: Google Analytics uses these cookies to collect data about how visitors interact with our Website. This helps us understand user behavior and improve our services.

### Managing Cookies

You have the right to disable cookies through your browser settings or by using content blockers. We respect your choice to use privacy protection tools and do not consider it a violation of our Terms of Service or Privacy Policy.

## Third-Party Links

Our Website may contain links to third-party websites. We do not control the privacy practices of these third-party sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies. We are not responsible for the content or practices of any linked websites.

## International Data Transfers

We do not store data ourselves but rely on third-party services (GitHub Pages and Google Analytics) for data storage. These third parties may transfer data internationally as part of their operations.

## Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. It is your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly. Your continued use of our Website after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that we have collected such information, please contact us to request deletion. We are committed to complying with all applicable laws regarding the protection of children's privacy.

## Complaints and Disputes

If you have any complaints or disputes regarding our handling of your personal data, please contact us at [contact@vorper.com](mailto:contact@vorper.com). We will work with you to address your concerns and resolve any issues.
